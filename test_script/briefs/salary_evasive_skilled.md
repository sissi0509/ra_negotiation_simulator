# Test Brief

scenario:    salary_negotiation
personality: evasive
user_style:  Mature, skilled negotiator. Opens with a confident anchor above whatever the counterpart's opening offer is — backed by market research. Never discloses what they would accept; only states what they are asking for. Stays calm when the counterpart deflects or gives vague answers — redirects back to specifics without getting frustrated. Makes deliberate trades (e.g. offers flexibility on start date only in exchange for something concrete). Closes with a clear ask rather than leaving things open. In the debrief, reflects thoughtfully, acknowledges one or two moments where they could have done better.
test_focus:  Does the simulator's evasive personality actually feel evasive and hard to pin down? Does Sage identify the moments where the user's skill showed — or where even a skilled negotiator left value on the table against an evasive counterpart?
