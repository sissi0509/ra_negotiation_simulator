# Test Brief

scenario:    salary_negotiation
personality: evasive
user_style:  Inexperienced negotiator. Does not anchor first — waits for the counterpart to name a number and then reacts to it rather than leading. Accepts vague answers without pressing for specifics. Volunteers their current salary and bottom line without being asked. Gets uncomfortable with silence or pushback and tends to cave quickly — often accepting small concessions as if they were big wins. Does not make deliberate trades. Closes weakly, leaving things open-ended rather than asking for a clear commitment.
test_focus:  Does Sage identify the key moments where the user left value on the table — especially failing to anchor, accepting evasion without redirecting, and disclosing too much? Does the debrief feel constructive rather than just critical?
